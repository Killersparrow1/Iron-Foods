# Multilingual Policy

Iron Foods aims to be the most comprehensive Indian food database.

## Supported Languages
- English (Primary)
- Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese.

## Submission Guidelines
- Provide the English name as the canonical identifier.
- Native names and aliases are encouraged in the `names` object.
- Transliteration should follow common usage to ensure searchability.

---

# Community Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on data quality and accuracy.
- Follow the [Review Process](REVIEW_PROCESS.md) for all contributions.

---

# Review Process

1. **Submission:** Contributor submits a PR.
2. **Automated Check:** CI runs schema validation and duplicate checks.
3. **Manual Review:** Maintainers review the data against the [Data Quality Policy](DATA_QUALITY_POLICY.md).
4. **Approval/Feedback:** Maintainers either approve, request changes, or decline the PR.
5. **Merge:** Approved PRs are merged.

---

# Maintainers

Maintainers are volunteers dedicated to maintaining the quality and health of the Iron Foods project.

- Maintainers hold write access to the repository.
- Responsibilities include reviewing PRs, managing issues, and enforcing policies.
