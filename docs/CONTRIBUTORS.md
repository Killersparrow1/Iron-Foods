# Contributors

We value every contribution! Iron Foods is built by the community for the community.

## Recognition
Contributors are recognized based on the scope and impact of their contributions.

### Contributor Levels
- **Level 1: Food Contributor** - Submitted 1-10 verified food items.
- **Level 2: Regional Contributor** - Submitted a full regional dataset (20+ items).
- **Level 3: Dataset Reviewer** - Actively reviewed and verified community submissions.
- **Level 4: Maintainer** - Long-term contributor with repository write access.
- **Level 5: Core Maintainer** - Responsible for architecture, policy, and overall project health.

## Badges & Recognition
- **Badges:** Contributors can display Iron Foods badges on their GitHub profile (coming soon).
- **Monthly Spotlight:** Every month, we highlight an outstanding contributor on our repository home page.
- **Hall of Fame:** A permanent list of our top contributors will be maintained in this file.
