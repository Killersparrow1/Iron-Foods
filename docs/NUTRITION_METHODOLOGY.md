# Nutrition Methodology

The Iron Foods database provides nutrition estimates for Indian food items.

## Methodology
- **Normalization:** Nutrition values are normalized per 100g of the food item in its specified state (e.g., raw, cooked).
- **Estimation:** Where institutional data is unavailable, we may rely on reputable community-sourced or derived data. These are explicitly tagged with `source_reliability: community`.
- **Source Prioritization:**
  1. IFCT (Indian Food Composition Tables)
  2. USDA FoodData Central
  3. Other government/institutional nutrition publications
  4. Academic research papers
  5. Community-contributed/derived estimates
- **Confidence Levels:** Data is categorized based on its source traceability, with higher confidence assigned to institutional data.

These values are estimates and should be treated as such for nutritional planning purposes.
